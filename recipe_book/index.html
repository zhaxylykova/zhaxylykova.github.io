Margarita Coctail

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Cazadores Tequila 1 part
Triple sec liqueur ½ part
Lime juice ½ part
Lime wedge 1
Salt for decoration
Cubed ice 1

Step 1: Chill your glass (the easiest way is to fill it with ice)

Step 2: Put lots of ice and all of the ingredients into a shaker, then shake hard for about 30 seconds to chill the liquid really well

Step 3: Run a lime wedge around the outside of the rim of your glass before rolling the rim in salt

Step 4: Double strain the mix into the glass

Pina Colada

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Pineapple 6-7 chunks
Sugar 1 teaspoon
Coconut cream 1 tablespoon
Bacardi Carta Blanco 50ml
Fresh pineapple juice 50ml
Ice as you wish

Step 1: Place all your ingredients into the blender (apart from the garnish!) and blend until a smooth consistency

Step 2: Pour into a chilled (or even better, frozen) glass, then add your garnish and serve

Blue-Hawaiian

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Bacardi Carta Blanco 50ml
Blue curaçao 50ml
Coconut cream 1 tsp
Pineapple juice 100ml

Step 1: Combine all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker and fill with ice. Shake then strain into a glass with cracked ice to serve.
